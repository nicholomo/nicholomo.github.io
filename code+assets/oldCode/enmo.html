Enmo

Product Line & Brand

This project began in 2018 and I continue to design and create handmade leather goods. I always love making things with my hands because it's an emotional and personal adventure when I design and iterate my ideas through hand-made methods. I fell in love with the natural aspect of hand cutting, binding, stitching, burnishing and presenting a full line of wallets and bags for a brand that I created. I Learned the natural characteristics of vegetable tanned and chromium tanned leather to design around constraints that prevented certain routs in the design process. Countless iterations of tech pack designs on Adobe Illustrator, color combinations for branding, logo designs, marketing strategies, failed prototypes, google advertisements and social media posts were the foundation of my Enmo leather brand.
