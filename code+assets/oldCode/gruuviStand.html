Gruuvi Stand

Portable Headphone Stand

I created this headphone stand concept as a three month intro to manufacturing school project. The prompt was to design a headphone stand for a desk that would be die cast in Zinc. My final idea was a portable work accessory that can be used elsewhere at coffee shops, backyards and other outdoor work environments rather than a stationary tool for the desk.
