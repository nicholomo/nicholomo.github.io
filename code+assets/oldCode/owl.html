OWL.

Client Project

A safety companion for backpackers and hikers to keep watch of dangerous wild animals during the day and night. The design incorporates a safety protocol, that when activated by a panic button, emits a 120 decibel alarm and records your surroundings with a 360 camera. Video footage is then sent to your phone via an OWL app with which you can then share with your friends after you bring yourself to safety.
